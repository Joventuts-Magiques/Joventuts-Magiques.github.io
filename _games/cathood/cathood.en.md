## Overview

Cathood is a cat-themed card game where players compete to create the happiest and most comfortable cat life. Collect toys, delicious food, and the best napping spots while avoiding vet visits!

## Components

- **Toy Cards**
- **Food Cards**
- **Sleeping Spot Cards**
- **Event Cards**
- **Happiness Markers**

## Objective

Achieve the maximum **happiness points** by collecting the best cards for your cat.

## Setup

1. **Shuffle all cards**
2. **Deal 5 cards** to each player
3. **Place the deck** in the center
4. **Each player starts with 0 points** of happiness

## How to Play

On your turn:

1. **Draw 1 card** from the deck
2. **Play 1 card** to your area:
   - **Toy**: +1-3 points
   - **Food**: +2-4 points
   - **Sleeping spot**: +3-5 points
3. **Apply special effects** if any

## Special Cards

### Toys

- **Ball of yarn**: +1 point, draw 1 card
- **Toy mouse**: +2 points
- **Laser**: +3 points, but discard 1 card

### Food

- **Kibble**: +2 points
- **Can of tuna**: +3 points, gain 1 extra turn
- **Milk**: +4 points

### Sleeping Spots

- **Cardboard box**: +3 points
- **Sofa**: +4 points
- **Sunbeam**: +5 points, but only if you have 2 toys

## Strategies

1. **Balance categories**: Don't focus on just one
2. **Save good cards**: For combos
3. **Watch opponents**: What they're collecting
4. **Use effects**: Maximize benefits

## Game End

The game ends when:

- The deck runs out
- A player reaches 30 points

The player with the most happiness points wins!

## Tips

1. **Cardboard boxes are valuable**: Cats love them
2. **Combine toys and food**: For bonuses
3. **Avoid the vet**: Negative event cards
4. **Play fast**: It's a light and fun game
