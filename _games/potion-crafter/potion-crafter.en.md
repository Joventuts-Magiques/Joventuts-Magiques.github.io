## Overview

Potion Crafter is an ingredient combination and resource management game where players take on the role of alchemists competing to create the most valuable and powerful potions.

## Components

- **Ingredient Cards** (Herbs, Minerals, Essences)
- **Recipe Cards**
- **Potion Markers**
- **Alchemy Board**
- **Gold Coins**

## Objective

Be the first alchemist to achieve **50 gold coins** by creating and selling potions.

## Setup

1. **Each player receives an alchemy board**
2. **Deal 5 ingredient cards** to each player
3. **Place 3 recipes** face-up in the market
4. **Each player starts with 10 coins**

## How to Play

On your turn, take 2 actions:

### Available Actions

1. **Gather Ingredients**
   - Draw 2 ingredient cards

2. **Buy Recipe**
   - Pay the cost and take a recipe from the market

3. **Craft Potion**
   - Discard required ingredients
   - Place the potion on your board

4. **Sell Potion**
   - Gain coins according to potion value

5. **Upgrade Laboratory**
   - Pay to unlock special abilities

## Types of Potions

### Basic Potions (5-10 coins)

- **Healing Potion**: 2 Herbs + 1 Water
- **Strength Potion**: 1 Mineral + 2 Essences

### Advanced Potions (15-25 coins)

- **Elixir of Life**: 3 Herbs + 2 Minerals + 1 Rare Essence
- **Invisibility Potion**: 2 Essences + 2 Special Minerals

### Master Potions (30-40 coins)

- **Philosopher's Stone**: Requires legendary ingredients
- **Elixir of Immortality**: The most valuable potion

## Special Ingredients

### Herbs

- **Common**: Easy to obtain
- **Rare**: Bonus effects
- **Legendary**: Very powerful

### Minerals

- **Crystals**: Amplifiers
- **Metals**: Stabilizers
- **Gems**: Premium ingredients

### Essences

- **Elemental**: Fire, Water, Earth, Air
- **Magical**: Light, Darkness
- **Divine**: Very rare

## Strategies

1. **Specialize**: Choose a potion type
2. **Manage resources**: Don't waste rare ingredients
3. **Upgrade laboratory**: Unlock combos
4. **Watch the market**: Which recipes are valuable
5. **Timing**: Sell when prices are high

## Laboratory Abilities

- **Distiller**: Purifies ingredients
- **Magic Cauldron**: Create 2 potions per turn
- **Library**: Store extra recipes
- **Herb Garden**: Generates herbs each turn

## Game End

The first player to reach **50 gold coins** wins immediately!

## Tips

1. **Diversify**: Don't depend on one ingredient type
2. **Plan ahead**: See which recipes you can complete
3. **Trade**: Exchange with other players
4. **Experiment**: Try new combinations
5. **Control timing**: Don't let others win first
