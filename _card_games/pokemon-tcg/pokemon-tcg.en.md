## Differences from Other TCGs

### vs Magic: The Gathering

- Simpler and more accessible
- Less interaction during opponent's turn
- More straightforward energy system
- Aimed at younger audience

### vs Yu-Gi-Oh

- Uses resources (energy) to attack
- Fewer complex combos
- Slower, more strategic pace
- Easier to learn
