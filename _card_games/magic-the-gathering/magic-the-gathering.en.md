## Overview

Magic: The Gathering (MTG) is a collectible card game created by Richard Garfield in 1993. Players take on the role of "planeswalkers" who cast spells, summon creatures, and use artifacts to defeat their opponents.

## Basic Components

### Card Types

- **Lands**: Generate mana, the resource needed to play other cards
- **Creatures**: Attack and defend
- **Spells**: Instant or permanent effects
- **Artifacts**: Magical objects with special abilities
- **Planeswalkers**: Powerful allies with unique abilities

## Objective

Reduce your opponent's life points from 20 to 0, or win through alternative conditions specified on some cards.

## Setup

1. Each player needs a **deck** of at least 60 cards
2. Shuffle your deck
3. Each player starts with **20 life points**
4. Draw 7 starting cards
5. Decide who goes first (coin flip or die roll)

### Mulligan

If you don't like your starting hand, you can "mulligan": shuffle your hand back into the deck, draw one fewer card, and repeat if necessary.

## How to Play

### Turn Structure

1. **Beginning Phase**
   - Untap your tapped cards
   - Draw a card

2. **Main Phase (First)**
   - Play a land (maximum one per turn)
   - Cast spells, creatures, artifacts
   - Activate abilities

3. **Combat Phase**
   - Declare attackers
   - Opponent declares blockers
   - Damage is resolved

4. **Main Phase (Second)**
   - Same as first main phase

5. **End Phase**
   - Discard down to 7 cards if you have more

### Combat

- **Attacking**: Tap the creatures you want to attack with
- **Blocking**: Opponent can block with their creatures without tapping
- **Damage**: Blocked and blocking creatures deal damage to each other
- **Unblocked damage**: Goes directly to opponent's life points

## Strategy

### Deck Building

- **Mana curve**: Balance the cost of your cards
- **Synergy**: Choose cards that work well together
- **Removal**: Include cards to eliminate threats
- **Card draw**: Keep your hand full of options

### During the Game

- **Resource management**: Don't spend all your mana every turn
- **Timing**: Some spells are better in response
- **Reading the opponent**: Watch their available mana
- **Protection**: Save answers for critical threats

## Popular Formats

### Standard

- Only cards from the last 2 years
- 60+ card decks
- Maximum 4 copies of each card (except basic lands)

### Commander (EDH)

- Exactly 100 card decks
- All cards unique (except basic lands)
- One legendary commander
- 40 starting life points
- Multiplayer games

### Modern

- Cards from 2003 onwards
- 60+ card decks
- Competitive format with great variety

## Tips for Beginners

1. **Start with a preconstructed deck**: Learn the basic mechanics
2. **Play with one or two colors**: Easier to manage mana
3. **Read cards carefully**: Keywords have specific meanings
4. **Practice timing**: Learn when to play instants
5. **Join the community**: Find local or online players

## History

Magic: The Gathering revolutionized card games when it launched in 1993. It created the collectible card game genre and continues to be the market leader with millions of players worldwide and a professional circuit with million-dollar prizes.
